What is Diversity and Inclusion?

Diversity

Diversity refers to the presence of differences within a given setting. In the workplace, it encompasses various characteristics that make individuals unique.

A diverse workforce is one that includes people from different walks of life, with varied perspectives and experiences.

Types of Diversity

There are many characteristics that contribute to diversity; inluding but not limited to:

  • Age
  • Gender Identity
  • Race
  • Sexual Orientation
  • Cultural Background
  • Physical Abilities

Inclusion

Inclusion is about creating an environment where all individuals feel respected, valued and supported.

It ensures that everyone, regardless of their background or identity, has equal access to opportunities, resources, and decision-making processes. While diversity focuses on who is in the workplace, inclusion is about how those individuals are integrated, empowered, and allowed to thrive.

Why be Inclusive?

It Benefits Employers

Prospective employees care about inclusion.

Businesses and organisations with strong diversity and inclusion policies are more attractive to prospective employees, so fostering a diverse and inclusive environment in your workplace will certainly benefit you and your business.
